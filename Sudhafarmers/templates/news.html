Changemaker: This technique used by a Punjab farmer helps stop stubble burning, reduces water requirement

Farmers' stir in Punjab

Farmers in Punjab are continuing their "rail roko" protest in Amritsar for the second day. They are demanding a committee for Minimum Support Price (MSP), a financial package for flood losses, relief from agricultural debt, and withdrawal of cases related to the Delhi agitation. The protest, led by the Kisan Mazdoor Sangharsh Committee, will last for three days. The protest has resulted in the cancellation and diversion of several trains in the area.
